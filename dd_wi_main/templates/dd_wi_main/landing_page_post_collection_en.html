This is a joint project of the Weizenbaum Institute Berlin and the University of Zurich. Journalists from Bayerischer Rundfunk and Stuttgarter Zeitung/ Stuttgarter Nachrichten analyze the data together with the research institutions. Together we find out which political posts and how many of them reach you on TikTok.

We thank all participants for their support!
